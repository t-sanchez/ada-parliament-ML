Swiss Parliament Voting Visualisation

How do the people from the National Council vote?

Deputees of the Parliament
This first approach details the votes that one deputee of the National Council made throughout his stay at the Parliament. It has two possible graphs, either aggregated by session, or at a single law level.
Votes at the Parliament
The second approeach details what is the topic of each law voted, and allows for a visualisation of the vote that each deputee made on a given law.

Inspired from the design of Adil Moujahid.
